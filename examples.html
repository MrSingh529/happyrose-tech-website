Invoice Generation Automation

Before

  • Each invoice created manually in Excel
  • ~2 hours spent per invoice to enter line items & taxes
  • High error rate leading to rework
  • Delays in sending invoices to clients

After

  • Automated reading of Excel line items
  • Intelligent tax calculations (CGST, SGST, IGST)
  • Multi‐line PDF invoices generated in seconds
  • Auto‐emails invoices & raw data to clients

By introducing a custom Python script and emailing workflow, we saved countless hours of repetitive invoicing work.

Approval Workflow Automation

Before

  • Confusion over who must approve requests
  • Manual back‐and‐forth emails for every permit
  • Hard to track approval status, leading to errors
  • No centralized record of tasks or attachments

After

  • Single Google Form collects all details & files
  • Auto‐generated approval emails with Approve/Reject links
  • Tracking ID & sheet logs statuses in real time
  • Eliminated confusion & drastically reduced delays

By integrating Google Forms, Sheets, and Apps Script, we built a seamless approval workflow that replaced manual requests with instant, transparent approvals.
